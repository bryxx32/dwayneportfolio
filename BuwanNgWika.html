Buwan ng Wika

What is the most important thing I learned from the event?

In this event, the most important thing I learned is a bit of history about the Philippines through our quiz bee and the country’s culture.

How can I apply what I have learned in real-life situations?

I can apply what I have learned by living out these lessons in real life — by respecting and loving the Philippine heritage as a proud Filipino.

Did I actively participate in the event? How?

I participated by joining classroom activities related to Buwan ng Wika and showing appreciation for our culture and language.

If I were to teach this topic or subject to a classmate, how would I explain it?

I would explain it by sharing what our culture means to us Filipinos and why events like Buwan ng Wika are important for understanding our heritage.

Why is it important to have an event (per subject)? Explain your answer.

It is important to have a Filipino subject event because we are Filipinos, and these events teach us to love our country, appreciate our culture, and understand our history. They encourage students to engage in cultural activities and apply what they learn in daily life.
