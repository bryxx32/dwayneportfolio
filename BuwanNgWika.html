Buwan Ng Wika

What is the most important thing I learned from the event?


In this event, the most important thing I learned is a slight history about the Philippines because of our history quiz and the country's culture.


How can I apply what i have learned in real life situations?


I can apply what I have learned by doing these things in real life and respecting and loving the Philippine heritage as a Filipino.


Did I actively participate in the event? How?


I can apply what I have learned by doing these things in real life and respecting and loving the Philippine heritage as a Filipino.


If i were to teach this topic/subject to a classmate, how would I explain it?


I will explain it by our countrys culture and what the importance of this event is to us Filipinos and what it might teach to us students.


Why is it important to have an event (per subject)? Explain your answer.


It is important to have an event in Filipino because simply said, we are Filipinos, and these events are what teaches us how to love our country and our culture and what our history is, encouraging students to apply what they learned in these events and engage in our culture.
